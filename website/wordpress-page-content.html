What It Does

Our AI-powered agent creates LinkedIn-ready content tailored to your brand. Give it a topic, get a complete post with copy, image brief, hashtags, and the best time to post.

  • Professional post copy (1300–2000 chars, LinkedIn-optimized)
  • Image brief or HTML template for visuals
  • 3–5 relevant hashtags + SEO keywords
  • Optimal posting time by industry & timezone
  • 10-minute reminder before post time
  • Response templates for comments & DMs

How It Works

2. Agent Generates

Gets new copy, image brief, hashtags, and optimal time — tailored to your company profile and target audience.

3. You Post

Review, approve, and post to LinkedIn. Manual or scheduled — you stay in control.
